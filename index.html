Who are we?

Real Gods Clan also known as RG is a gaming clan which was established in May 2019. We are an International Mobile Gaming Clan which includes games such as Valorant and Call of Duty Mobile.

What do we do?

We are a clan, a tournament organization, a team and a community of gamers from all over the world. We at Real Gods Clan provide the best management and the perfect gaming experience for gamers.
