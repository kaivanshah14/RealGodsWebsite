About Us

Real Gods Esports is an Indian Esports Team & Tournament Organization for THE FINALS, Valorant and Call of Duty Mobile. Established in May 2019, we have focused on creating a community of gaming enthusiasts with one goal - to bring gamers worldwide under one roof to encourage the upcoming talent in the Esports field. We have created more than 50+ teams for various games and have hosted 5 major public tournaments. We currently host a staff team with more than 25 members working in different domains to ensure the upbringing of the Organization. We have a Discord community of more than 2800+ members and are continuously growing. We have a dedicated team for content creation, social media management, and marketing to ensure the best experience for our community members.

Our Vision: Our vision is to create a space where talent is nurtured, gaming skills are respected, and members of the organization are recognized throughout the Indian gaming community. A place where gamers lead, manage, organize, thrive, and grow together as part of RG. We wish to make RG an organization for those passionate about gaming as players, content creators, community managers, team leaders, or community members.
