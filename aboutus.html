About Real Gods Clan

Real Gods Clan is a Gaming Clan under the North American, European and Asian (Indian) Region

〢〄 History

Real Gods Clan also known as "RG" in short started it's history in 2019. The Clan was formely created by ToeTag in May'19. The Clan had Bullet Force as it's primary game with a successful lineup under Europe (EU) Region which was known widely throughout the Bullet Force community. Real Gods then migrated to Call of Duty Mobile also known as CODM in the month of December 2019. The clan gradually made progress and built their first ever lineup for CODM under North American (NA) region, followed by two lineups under EU region. The clan then commenced it's journey under the Asian Region with it's Indian Lineup coming in mid March 2020. Throughout the journey of CODM, Real Gods have formed nearly 10 lineups for Multiplayer Mode and 2 lineups for Battle Royale Mode for CODM. Each lineup brought in new players, new managers making the journey even more vivid and exciting, the clan has received 1000+ applications till date for both Competitve and Casual players. The clan survived through the unity between the players and the Staff Team working at Real Gods, through which a virtual home for players all around the world was made possible.
